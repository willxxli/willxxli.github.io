## Working Papers

### The Impact of Indexing on Mutual Fund Performance
*Working Paper*

**Data:** US domestic equity mutual funds
**Methods:** Recursive de-meaned estimator

This paper empirically examines the effects of indexing on mutual fund performance. Key findings include:
- The absence of market timing abilities among active mutual fund managers
- A negative relationship between fund size and performance

## Research Projects

### Institutional Background of Asset Management Industry
*April 2024 - Present*

**Data:** SEC filings
**Methods:** LLM, Textual Analysis

This project leverages advanced AI technologies to understand the institutional structure of the asset management industry:
- Utilize LLMs and GPT API services to construct textual data from regulatory filings
- Develop AI-based agents and automated workflows to systematically fetch and process data on a daily basis
- Apply natural language processing techniques to extract insights from unstructured financial documents
