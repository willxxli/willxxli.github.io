Will holds a Bachelor of Commerce (Finance and Accounting) from the University of Queensland and a Master of Finance from the University of Melbourne. Will is currently a PhD candidate in Finance at The University of Queensland Business School, working under the supervision of [Dr. Min Zhu](https://about.uq.edu.au/experts/21032) and [Prof. Denis Leung](https://economics.smu.edu.sg/research/economic-policy-influence/prof-denis-leung) (Singapore Management University).

He combines academic research with professional expertise in finance and investment. He holds the **Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA)** designation and has written extensively on topics related to money management, macroeconomic developments, and trading philosophies. Will has been teaching and presenting Top 3 Finance and Economics Journals including (JF, JFE, RFS, AER, QJE, JPE) on his own social media channel (in Mandarin).

**Research Interests:** Empirical Asset Pricing, Behavioural Finance, Financial Market, Investment, Asset Management, CryptoCurrency, AI Applications in Finance

**Technical Skills:** Python, R, LaTeX, Econometrics, Database Management, LLM/AI Applications
